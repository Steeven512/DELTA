DELTA

General client interface engineered for the management of regulated stablecoins.

provides users with tools to easy interact with regulated stable coins ERC-20 by synchronizing with
Ethereum-based blockchain RPCs to request and index specific events within a local database for
efficient access and tracing, alongside the integration of wallet management and transaction handling
capabilities.
